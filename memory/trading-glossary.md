# Trading Glossary for Gorka

Terms Fabian uses that need clarification:

## Acronyms
- **HTF** - High Time Frame (daily, weekly charts)
- **LTF** - Low Time Frame (1m, 5m, 15m charts)
- **PB** - Pullback (price retraces before continuing)
- **TP** - Take Profit
- **SL** - Stop Loss
- **ATM** - At The Money (market order)
- **POC** - Point of Control (highest volume price level)
- **MegaPOC** - Major POC level, significant support/resistance

## VWAP Levels
- **+1 / -1** - One standard deviation from VWAP
- **+1.5 / -1.5** - 1.5 standard deviations from VWAP
- **+2 / -2** - Two standard deviations from VWAP

## Fabian's Rules
- "No touching ±2, no entry" - Need price to reach ±2 level before considering a trade
- "Close past 1.5 first" - Wait for a candle to close beyond the 1.5 level
- "+1/-1 retests" - Enter on pullback to ±1 after price extends beyond

## Instruments
- **ES** - E-mini S&P 500 futures
- **MNQ** - Micro E-mini Nasdaq futures
- **NQ** - E-mini Nasdaq futures

## Setups
- "Arms bullish/bearish" - Setup is ready, waiting for trigger
- "Retest" - Price returns to a level after breaking it
